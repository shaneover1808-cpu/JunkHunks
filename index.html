FAQ

What items can’t you take?

We can’t take hazardous materials like Asbestos or large preassurised tanks. We’re happy to point you to proper disposal options.

Do you recycle?

We recycle cardboard and similar paper products. Good‑condition items are donated when possible.

Do I need to move items to the curb?

Nope. We handle all lifting and loading from wherever the items are.
